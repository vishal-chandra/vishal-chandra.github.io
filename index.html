Harmonograph

A simulation of a Harmonograph table, a suspended, swinging table with a pen fixed to it. The table acts like a two dimensional pendulum, so the images can recreated by plotting two parametric equations.

Flappy Genetics

Exploring genetic algorthims by teaching flappy bird to play by itself! Following the model of genetic algorithms, the leaders of each "generation" are used as the basis of the next generation. Implemented from scratch to optimize the weights of Kim Marcel's Toy Neural Net.
